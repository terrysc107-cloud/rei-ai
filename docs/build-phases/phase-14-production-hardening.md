# Phase 14 - Production Hardening

## Purpose

Make the application stable, safe, accessible, and ready for real-world use.

## Why This Phase Matters

A product can feel complete from a feature perspective and still be too fragile or rough to launch.

## Scope

- error handling
- loading states
- validation
- route protection
- accessibility
- metadata
- deployment quality

## Required Work

1. Add strong loading, empty, and error states.
2. Tighten form validation and edge-case handling.
3. Improve accessibility and keyboard/navigation behavior.
4. Add metadata, SEO, OG images, and share-ready polish.
5. Confirm deployment health and environment handling.
6. Add analytics hooks for core product events.

## Deliverables

- stronger resilience and polish
- launch-safe interactions
- deployable production quality

## Dependencies

- major product and backend flows should already be in place

## Definition Of Done

- the product feels reliable and intentional
- there are no obvious unhandled edges
- the app can be shared confidently with real users

## Risks And Notes

- skipping this phase creates hidden trust damage even if the product looks good
