# Phase 15 - Launch Readiness

## Purpose

Prepare the product to be shared, reviewed, sold, and iterated on as a real offering.

## Why This Phase Matters

The final phase is not just technical. It is where positioning, usability, business clarity, and confidence all have to align.

## Scope

- final experience review
- shareability
- business clarity
- QA
- launch packaging

## Required Work

1. Confirm the flagship user journey is genuinely strong.
2. Confirm one or more complete tracks are launch-worthy.
3. Review the pricing and custom build paths for trust and clarity.
4. Make sure the app is easy to demo and share.
5. Run a final QA pass across public, learner, and service surfaces.

## Deliverables

- launch-ready product
- final product review checklist
- confidence that the app is ready for real users

## Dependencies

- all major product, content, monetization, and backend phases should be effectively complete

## Definition Of Done

- the app can be shared publicly without apology
- the learner path, prompt system, project path, and service funnel all feel coherent
- the product looks and behaves like a real business, not a prototype

## Risks And Notes

- do not confuse “live” with “ready”
- a launchable product needs trust, not just features
